How Wobb Works

Create a campaign

From the dashboard, click on “Create Campaign”. Simply explain your marketing objective to the Influencers via an easy to understand and fill form. More than 10 advanced criteria are available to narrow down your search and perform targeted campaigns.

Discover Influencers

Based on the brief and objective shared by you, Wobb ML engine finds you best fit Influencers across any platform of choice.

Vetting of influencers

Our Influencers go through our proprietary fraud detection test. Fake followers and paid engagement is removed during price calculation so you stop overpaying them every time and rake in profit!

Standardized Prices

Further prices are standardized for each Influencer so you don’t waste time negotiating.
